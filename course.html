Our Courses

Courses We Offer

At Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, we provide a wide range of academic programs designed to meet the needs of students at every stage of their educational journey. From intermediate courses to post-graduate degrees, our programs focus on nurturing academic excellence, creativity, and innovation.

Intermediate

Our intermediate programs are ideal for students looking to build foundational knowledge in fields such as science, business, and social studies. These programs help bridge the gap between secondary education and university-level degrees, preparing students for further academic or professional pursuits. Whether you aim to gain specific skills or explore diverse academic fields, our intermediate courses offer flexibility and a strong academic grounding.

Degree

SUSL offers a variety of undergraduate degree programs across five faculties, including Agricultural Sciences, Applied Sciences, Management Studies, Social Sciences and Languages, and Technology. Our degree programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring students are well-prepared to excel in their chosen careers. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we are committed to helping students achieve their full potential.

Post Graduation

For those looking to further their academic pursuits or specialize in their fields, we offer comprehensive post-graduate programs, including Masters and Ph.D. degrees. These programs are designed for students who are ready to dive deep into research and innovation, contributing to cutting-edge advancements in their industries. Our post-graduate students work alongside expert faculty members on projects that tackle both local and global challenges.

Our Facilities

At Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, we take pride in offering top-notch facilities to support our students' academic, athletic, and personal development. Our world-class infrastructure and services ensure that students have everything they need to thrive during their time at SUSL.

World Class Library

Our library is one of the most comprehensive academic libraries in the country, providing students access to thousands of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources. The library offers dedicated study areas, research support services, and online databases to enhance learning. Whether you're preparing for exams or conducting in-depth research, our library is an invaluable resource for your academic success.

Larget Playground

We are proud to have one of the largest and most versatile playgrounds in the region. Our state-of-the-art sports facilities cater to a wide range of activities, including cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and more. Regular sports events and training sessions help students stay fit and healthy while fostering a spirit of teamwork and competition. With both outdoor and indoor facilities, we ensure year-round access to sports and recreational activities.

About us

Established in 1995, Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka (SUSL) stands as one of the most beautiful and dynamic institutions of higher education in the country. Nestled in the picturesque foothills of the Sabaragamuwa Province, we offer world-class education paired with a vibrant campus life. Our diverse programs, cutting-edge research initiatives, and commitment to excellence help shape future leaders ready to make a difference. Explore our rich academic offerings, innovative projects, and engaging extracurricular activities that prepare our students for the modern world. Discover a place where learning meets nature, and where students thrive in an inclusive, dynamic environment.
