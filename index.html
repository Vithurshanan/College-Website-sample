Our Facilities

At Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, we take pride in offering top-notch facilities to support our students' academic, athletic, and personal development. Our world-class infrastructure and services ensure that students have everything they need to thrive during their time at SUSL.

World Class Library

Our library is one of the most comprehensive academic libraries in the country, providing students access to thousands of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources. The library offers dedicated study areas, research support services, and online databases to enhance learning. Whether you're preparing for exams or conducting in-depth research, our library is an invaluable resource for your academic success.

Larget Playground

We are proud to have one of the largest and most versatile playgrounds in the region. Our state-of-the-art sports facilities cater to a wide range of activities, including cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and more. Regular sports events and training sessions help students stay fit and healthy while fostering a spirit of teamwork and competition. With both outdoor and indoor facilities, we ensure year-round access to sports and recreational activities.

Tasty and healty Food

Our on-campus dining facilities offer a variety of nutritious and delicious meals that cater to different tastes and dietary preferences. From traditional Sri Lankan dishes to international cuisine, our canteens and food courts serve meals that are not only tasty but also healthy. We prioritize fresh ingredients and balanced meals to support students’ physical well-being, ensuring that everyone can enjoy wholesome food during their time at SUSL.

About Us

Founded in 1995, Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka (SUSL) is one of the premier higher education institutions in the country.
Nestled in the scenic foothills of the Sabaragamuwa Province, the university is committed to providing world-class education while embracing the cultural and natural heritage of Sri Lanka.

Vision and Mission

SUSL strives to be a beacon of excellence in higher education, research, and community service. Our mission is to foster academic rigor, creativity, and innovation, empowering students to become leaders who contribute to the social, cultural, and economic development of Sri Lanka and beyond.

Academic Excellence

SUSL offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across its five faculties:

1. Faculty of Agricultural Sciences

2. Faculty of Applied Sciences

3. Faculty of Management Studies

4. Faculty of Social Sciences and Languages

5. Faculty of Technology

Each faculty is driven by a commitment to academic excellence, equipped with modern facilities, and staffed by a talented faculty who are dedicated to guiding students toward their personal and professional goals.

Research and Innovation

At SUSL, we believe in the power of research to drive progress. Our university is at the forefront of innovation, with ongoing projects in fields such as sustainable agriculture, environmental science, business management, and social sciences. We encourage students and faculty alike to pursue interdisciplinary research that addresses global challenges while remaining relevant to local needs.

Campus Life

Our campus offers a vibrant and inclusive environment, where students from diverse backgrounds come together to learn and grow. Beyond academics, SUSL promotes a healthy balance between studies and extracurricular activities, with numerous clubs, sports teams, and cultural events enriching campus life. Our beautiful surroundings also offer plenty of opportunities for outdoor recreation and community engagement.

Why Choose SUSL?

Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka is committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are ready to meet the challenges of the modern world. We are proud of our graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields both locally and internationally. Choosing SUSL means becoming part of a dynamic academic community dedicated to making a difference.
